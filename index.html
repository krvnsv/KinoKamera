Papa Films

Productions

Papa Films is a Serbian, Belgrade-based production company that was founded in 2009 by writer/director Darko Lungulov. Films produced by Papa Films are international coproductions supported by Eurimages and MEDIA funds, screened at international festivals (Tribeca, Karlovy Vary, Goteborg, Sarajevo) and broadcasted on channels/platforms such as Netflix, HBO, Amazon Prime.

Papa Films nourishes long-standing relations with Films Boutique a renowned World sale that distributed their previous movies and is on board to represent Papa Films' next one, the "1970".

Filmography

HERE AND THERE (2009), Serbian-USA-German coproduction, Tribeca 2009 winner, screened on Netflix from 2010-2012, won 20 awards at over 50 festivals, currently streaming on Amazon Prime.

MONUMENT TO MICHAEL JACKSON (2014), a Serbian-German-Croatian-Macedonian coproduction supported by Eurimages and MEDIA, premiered at the Karlovy Vary and broadcasted on HBO Eastern Europe.

DEATH IN CATHEDRAL (2020), a Croatian–Serbian coproduction supported by MEDIA, premiered at Annecy, the world's most important animated film festival. Subsequently, in 2021 and 2022, it screened at more than 40 international festivals, including Tokyo, Animafest, Sitges, Bruxelles, and Bordeaux, amongst others.

ELECTRIC YU-TOPIA (YU GRUPA – TRENUTAK SNA) (2023), a music documentary - IDFA 2023 Docs For Sale selection, was voted the Best Documentary in 2023 by the Film Artists Serbia Association (UFUS) and garnered three awards at the 70th Belgrade Documentary Film Festival: Grand Prix, Best Editing, and Best Sound Design Award. Darko Lungulov has won a prestigious annual City of Belgrade Award for Contribution to Film and TV for this film in 2023.
