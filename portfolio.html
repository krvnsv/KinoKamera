YU GRUPA

DOCUMENTARY   |   RELEASED 2023

Original title: YU Grupa - Trenutak sna

Director: Darko Lungulov

Screenwriter: Darko Lungulov

The three Jelic brothers were born right after WW II, together with the new Yugoslavia. As they were growing up, rock'n'roll came from “the West.” The brothers formed a rock band and named it after Yugoslavia - The Yu Grupa. Ultimately, Yugoslavia broke up in bloody civil war. The band outlived the doomed country. The film thematizes the moments in the history of Yugoslavia, weaving them together with the brothers' personal struggles using intimate Super 8 home films blended with Yugoslav Film Archives footage spanning decades, collapsing barriers between epic and intimate, past and present, war and peace.

THE ADVENTURES OF GLORIA SCOTT: MURDER IN THE CATHEDRAL

MYSTERY DRAMA   |   RELEASED 2020

Original title: The Adventures of Gloria Scott: Murder in the Cathedral

Director:Matija Pisacic, Tvrtko Raspolic

Screenwriter:Matija Pisacic, Mima Simic, Jasna Zmak

In this gripping mystery, Gloria Scott, a brilliant young detective, investigates a chilling murder inside a historic cathedral. As she unravels the secrets of the clergy and the town’s elite, Gloria discovers a web of deceit tied to a decades-old scandal. Blending atmospheric tension with intricate character studies, the film captures the eerie beauty of the cathedral through stunning cinematography, merging past betrayals with present dangers in a race against time.

ESCAPE

SHORT FILM   |   RELEASED 2004

Original title: Bekstvo

Director: Darko Lungulov

Screenwriter: Darko Lungulov

In 1999, during US led NATO bombing in former Yugoslavia, Ljilja and her two sons escape Belgrade to ironically seek refuge in the US. Over the course of the next four years, filmmaker follows this single mother, whose bitterness toward US foreign policy is still palpable, and her two sons as they make a new home in America. The film focuses largely on the sons, Vlada (11) and Dusan (8) as they navigate the complicated waters of “becoming American”. We follow them through their complex quest for personal and national identity. Filmed over 5 years, ESCAPE delves deep into global issues, and yet remains an intensely personal story.

1970

FEATURE FILM   |   IN DEVELOPMENT

Original title:1970

Director: Darko Lungulov

Screenwriter: Darko Lungulov

Drasko, a 13-years old boy living in Belgrade, is spending his school vacation during the hot summer of 1970, at his grandparents in Vojvodina. There he meets Erzika, who introduces him to the thriving hippy culture and music. When he accidentally learns that his parents intend to leave him there due to the crisis of their marriage, a devastated Drasko runs away with Erzika, to join the upcoming anti-Vietnam war protests in Belgrade, and to return back home.
